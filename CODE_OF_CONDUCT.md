# Code of Conduct

This project follows the **[Contributor Covenant 2.1][cc]**.

In short: be respectful, assume good intent, and keep discussion focused on
the work. Harassment, personal attacks, or persistent hostility are not
welcome and will get you removed from the project's spaces.

## Enforcement

Report problems privately to **pandaleecn@gmail.com** with `[cc-dashboard conduct]`
in the subject line. Reports are kept confidential. Responses may include a
warning, removal of the offending content, or a ban from the project — at my
discretion as the sole maintainer.

This is a hobby project maintained in spare time. I reserve the right to
close off-topic or hostile threads without discussion.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
